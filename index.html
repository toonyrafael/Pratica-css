Voyager 1

Voyager 1 is a space probe launched by NASA on September 5, 1977. It is part of the Voyager program to study the outer Solar System and interstellar space beyond the Sun's heliosphere. As of now, Voyager 1 is the farthest human-made object from Earth, having entered interstellar space in August 2012.
